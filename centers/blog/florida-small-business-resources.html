📍 Florida

Free Small Business Resources in Florida — Complete 2025 Guide

By SMBProFind Team · Updated 2025 · 10 min read

Florida's diverse economy — spanning tourism, agriculture, international trade, defense, and real estate — is backed by a strong network of free small business resources. The Florida SBDC Network is consistently rated one of the best in the country, and the state's proximity to Latin America makes it a hub for export assistance programs.

1. Florida SBDC Network

The Florida SBDC Network, hosted by the University of West Florida, operates 40+ service centers statewide. Florida's SBDC is particularly well-regarded for three specialties:

  • Disaster recovery — Florida SBDCs have extensive experience helping businesses recover from hurricanes and floods
  • Government contracting — Florida has major defense and federal contracting opportunities
  • International trade & export — Given Florida's location, SBDC has strong export counseling programs

Find your nearest center at floridasbdc.org

2. SCORE Florida Chapters

  • SCORE South Florida — Miami, Broward, Palm Beach
  • SCORE Greater Orlando — Tourism, hospitality, tech
  • SCORE Tampa Bay — Finance, healthcare, manufacturing
  • SCORE Jacksonville — Logistics, finance, military
  • SCORE Southwest Florida — Fort Myers, Naples area

3. Women's Business Centers in Florida

  • WBDC Florida — Miami — export, counseling, training for women entrepreneurs
  • Prospera — Orlando — bilingual (English/Spanish) counseling for Hispanic women
  • Florida Small Business Development Center WBC Programs — statewide women's programs

4. Veterans Business Resources

  • VBOC at University of West Florida — Pensacola — serving all of Florida
  • Boots to Business — Available at Florida's many military installations
  • Florida Veterans Foundation — State-level veteran business grants and support
  • Tampa Bay VBOC — Serving central and south Florida veterans

🎖️ Florida has one of the largest veteran populations in the US. The VBOC at UWF is consistently rated one of the top VBOCs in the country for veteran entrepreneur support.

5. Enterprise Florida & State Programs

Enterprise Florida is the state's public-private partnership for economic development, focused on:

  • Export assistance and international trade missions
  • Foreign direct investment attraction
  • Industry cluster development (aerospace, life sciences, financial services)
  • Market development grants for exporters

The Florida DEO also runs several small business programs including community development block grants, rural programs, and workforce training incentives.

6. Disaster Recovery Resources

Florida's exposure to hurricanes means disaster recovery is a critical resource category:

  • SBA Disaster Loans — Up to $2M for businesses affected by declared disasters
  • Florida SBDC Disaster Recovery Program — Free consulting for storm-impacted businesses
  • Florida Commerce Rebuild Florida — State grants for hurricane recovery
  • FEMA Business Recovery Resources — Federal assistance programs

7. Metro-Specific Resources

Miami & South Florida

  • Beacon Council — Miami-Dade economic development and international business
  • Miami-Dade Empowerment Trust — minority business loans and support
  • SBA Miami District Office — loans and certifications

Orlando & Central Florida

  • GrowFL — second-stage business growth programs
  • Orlando Economic Partnership — business development and site selection
  • CFE Federal Credit Union — small business lending

Tampa Bay

  • Tampa Bay EDC — regional business development
  • Tampa Bay Chamber — networking and small business advocacy
  • Hillsborough County Economic Development — local business incentives
