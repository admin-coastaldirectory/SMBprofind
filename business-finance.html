📚 Must-Read Books

📗
Profit First (Book)
The foundational book for small business financial management — set up bank accounts that enforce profitability automatically.
Book
📙
Profit First
Mike Michalowicz's cash management system for small businesses — take profit first before paying expenses. The formula: Sales − Profit = Expenses. Practical, immediately applicable, and transformative for business owners who are always busy but never profitable. Essential read for any SMB owner.
Book
📊
Simple Numbers (Greg Crabtree)
Greg Crabtree's no-nonsense guide to using financial data to grow a profitable small business. Introduces 'simple numbers' benchmarks for labor efficiency, profit targets, and capital management that every owner-operator should track. Written for business owners, not accountants.
Book
