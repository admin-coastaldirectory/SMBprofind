Our Story

SMB ProFind was created to solve a real problem: small business owners, entrepreneurs, and operators don't have a trusted, curated place to find the influencers, educators, and content creators who speak directly to their world.

Unlike influencer databases built for enterprise brands, ProFind is built for the SMB community and by people who understand it. Every profile is manually reviewed. Every category is built around the real challenges SMB operators face every day.

Our Principles

  • No pay-to-play. You cannot buy your way into ProFind. Every listing is earned.
  • Verified, not scraped. Every influencer profile has been manually reviewed for accuracy.
  • Independent. Our editorial decisions are not influenced by affiliate relationships or sponsorships.
  • Transparent. We disclose every affiliate relationship clearly and prominently.
