Our Story

SMBProFind was created to solve a real problem: small business owners, entrepreneurs, and operators don't have a trusted, curated place to find the influencers, educators, and content creators who speak directly to their world.

Unlike influencer databases built for enterprise brands, SMBProFind is built for the SMB community and by people who understand it. Every profile is manually reviewed. Every category is built around the real challenges SMB operators face every day.

Whether you run an HVAC company, manage a franchise, sell online, or are buying your first business — there's a category and a set of educators here that speak your language.

Our Principles

  • No pay-to-play. You cannot buy your way into SMBProFind. Every listing is earned based on the value the educator delivers to the SMB community.
  • Verified, not scraped. Every influencer profile has been manually reviewed for accuracy. We don't auto-populate from social APIs.
  • Independent. Our editorial decisions are not influenced by affiliate relationships or sponsorships. Affiliate links are clearly disclosed.
  • Transparent. We disclose every affiliate relationship clearly and prominently. Amazon links use tag: smbinf-20.
  • SMB-focused. We don't list generic motivational speakers. Every educator in our directory has content specifically relevant to small business operators.
