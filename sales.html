📚 Must-Read Books

📘
To Sell is Human
Daniel Pink's research-backed guide to modern selling — persuasion, pitching, and the psychology of moving people to act.
Book
📕
$100M Offers (Alex Hormozi)
Alex Hormozi's framework for creating offers so good people feel stupid saying no. Covers value equation, pricing, bonuses, guarantees, and naming — teaching any business owner how to charge premium prices and eliminate price resistance. One of the most-shared business books of the last 5 years.
Book
🎯
Never Split the Difference
Former FBI hostage negotiator Chris Voss's negotiation playbook — tactical empathy, mirroring, calibrated questions, and the accusation audit. Immediately applicable to sales calls, vendor negotiations, lease renewals, and employee conversations. One of the most practically useful books for any business owner.
Book
📖
Predictable Revenue (Book)
Aaron Ross and Marylou Tyler's foundational book on outbound sales systems, including the Cold Calling 2.0 methodology that generated $100M in ARR at Salesforce. Required reading for anyone building a B2B sales team.
Book
📖
The Sales Acceleration Formula (Book)
Mark Roberge's data-driven playbook for hiring, training, and scaling a SaaS-era B2B sales team. The first book to apply a scientific, metrics-based framework to every stage of the sales machine.
Book
📖
Sell or Be Sold (Book)
Grant Cardone's foundational book on the sales mindset — arguing that everything in life is a sale and that learning to sell is the most important skill any entrepreneur or salesperson can develop.
Book
📖
The Little Red Book of Selling (Book)
Jeffrey Gitomer's 12.5 Principles of Sales Greatness — a pocket-sized masterclass on customer loyalty, referral generation, and why people buy. One of the bestselling sales books of all time for consumer-facing reps.
Book
📖
The Sales Development Playbook (Book)
Trish Bertuzzi's definitive guide to building a high-performing SDR team for SaaS companies. Covers hiring profiles, compensation structures, metrics, sequencing strategy, and how to align sales development with marketing.
Book
📖
Selling in the OR (Mace Horoff)
Mace Horoff's practical guide to the unique challenges of surgical and operating room sales — covering how to build relationships with surgeons, navigate hospital politics, and sell complex medical devices in clinical settings.
Book
