📚 Must-Read Books

📗
The E-Myth Revisited
Michael Gerber's seminal book on why most small businesses fail — and how to fix it. The core insight: business owners must work on the business, not just in it. Introduces the Franchise Prototype model and the three personalities every owner must manage: entrepreneur, manager, and technician.
Book
📘
Company of One (Paul Jarvis)
A counter-narrative to growth-at-all-costs — Paul Jarvis argues that staying lean, independent, and focused can lead to more profit, freedom, and sustainability than constant expansion. Must-read for solopreneurs and lifestyle business owners who want to optimize for margin, not headcount.
Book

How I Built This (NPR)
Guy Raz's legendary NPR podcast interviewing the founders of the world's best-known companies. From Airbnb to Spanx, each episode dives deep into origin stories, near-death moments, and the decisions that made the difference. Required listening for any entrepreneur.
Podcast

My First Million
Sam Parr and Shaan Puri brainstorm business ideas, break down how companies got to $1M+ in revenue, and riff on trends before they hit. One of the most entertaining and intellectually rich shows in the entrepreneurship space — famous for deep-dive idea breakdowns.
