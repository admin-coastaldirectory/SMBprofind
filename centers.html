Your Complete Guide to Free Small Business Resources in America

This page is a searchable directory of free and low-cost government-backed small business resources available across all 50 states. Most small business owners don't know these exist — yet they provide the same quality of business planning, financial, and marketing advice you'd pay a consultant thousands of dollars for.

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) funds a nationwide network of resource partners including Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs) with nearly 1,000 locations offering free one-on-one counseling; SCORE with 250+ chapters of volunteer executive mentors; Women's Business Centers (WBCs) serving women entrepreneurs; and Veterans Business Outreach Centers (VBOCs) supporting military-connected founders. All services listed here are either completely free or very low cost.

Use the search and filters below to find resources in your state, city, or by the type of help you need — whether that's writing a business plan, applying for an SBA loan, finding a mentor, or accessing minority business programs. Once you find free local help, come back to SMBProFind's influencer directory to find coaches and educators who go deeper on the same topics.
